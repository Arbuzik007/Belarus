Description

Food

A significant part of the national Belarusian cuisine consists of dishes made from grated potatoes: potato pancakes, dumplings, sorcerers, potato casseroles, babka.

Languages

Belarusian and Russian are the official languages ​​of Belarus. This happened due to Belarus joining first Russia, then the USSR

Our neighbours

Belarus borders on five countries: in the west – with Poland, in the northwest – with Lithuania, in the north – with Latvia, in the east – with Russia, in the south – with Ukraine.

Main towns

Vitebsk

Almost 400 thousand people live here. The city is famous for the Slavic Bazaar. And the main attraction is the famous artist Marc Chagall, who was born here.

Hrodna

One of the oldest and most beautiful cities in Belarus. Castols and ancient churches, medieval castles and many ancient buildings make up its architecture.

Minsk

This is the capital of the Republic of Belarus. Almost 2 million people live here. The city is rich in attractions, beautiful buildings, modern shopping centers and recreational areas.

Mogilev

It is called the second capital of Belarus, but much fewer people live here than in Minsk - 350 thousand people. After the war, there are few sights left here

Brest

This is a city with a rich history. People come here to see the legendary Brest Fortress, take a walk in the Belovezhskaya Pushcha nature reserve and explore ancient castles in the vicinity of the city.

Homiel

It is the second largest city in Belarus and one of the most popular tourist cities. The name “Gomel” comes from the name of the Gomeyuk stream, which flowed into the Sozh River

Our natural

Bison

The bison is a symbol of Belarus. It is the heaviest and largest mammal in Europe. Its weight is 450-640 kg, maximum 920 kg.Despite its large mass, the bison runs very quickly

Lynx

The common lynx is a large wild cat. There are long tassels on the ears. The tail is short with a “chopped off” end (20-40 cm). Elongated hair on the sides - “whiskers”. The muzzle is short, the eyes are wide, the pupils are round. Lynx has sharp eyesight.

Eagle

The body length of the white-tailed eagle is from 70 to 90 centimeters, the wingspan is from 200 to 230 centimeters, and the weight is from 4 to 7 kilograms. They feed on fish or other water-related animals.

Bear

The brown bear has a large round head with an elongated nose, small ears, and a short and thick neck. The powerful paws have 5 toes that end in sharp and long claws. Coat color - dark brown shades

Stork

The white stork is the most famous of the storks. It is a white bird with black wing tips, a long neck, a long, thin, red beak and long reddish legs.
